Section 1 of 8

How should events show up in your calendar?

When you schedule Job 999 with All Floorz on a Building project, where should that event appear?

Recommended Option A: Trade calendar only
The event goes on the Flooring calendar only. To see all Building work, you'd toggle on the trade calendars Building uses.

Pros: One event per job, less clutter.
Cons: Filtering by division means toggling multiple trade calendars.
Option B: Both trade AND division calendar
The event is created twice: once on Flooring, once on Building. You can toggle "Building" to see all Building work at a glance.

Pros: Easy filter by division. Color stays on trade calendar.
Cons: Twice as many events, double-edits if you reschedule.
